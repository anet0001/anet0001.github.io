About Me

My Name is Nonso and I am a first year Interactive media design student at Algonquin College, Ottawa. I am Nigerian and I was born on the 18th of May, 2001. I am currently in my first semester at Algonquin College and I am loving it so far. I think its a good school and I am curious to see what the future holds for me here.

I love to code, watch football (not soccer -_-) and play video games in my free time. I also spend time recreating designs I see on awwwards with Next.js and GSAP.
